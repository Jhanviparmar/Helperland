FAQS

Whether you are Customer or Service provider, We have tried our best to solve all your queries and questions.

  • FOR CUSTOMER
  • FOR SERVICE PROVIDER

What is included in basic cleaning?

Living room, bedroom and bathroom, kitchen and common rooms

Which helper comes to me?

Helperland has an extensive network of experienced cleaning staff. Depending on the location, date and duration, we will try to assign you the best available helper. If a cleaner accepts your order, you will receive an email with the profile of the helper. If you use our service more often, you can save a cleaner as a favorite. For future bookings, your favorite will be requested first.

Can I skip or move bookings?

You can change a booking free of charge 48 hours before an assignment. If you want to skip an assignment that has already been booked, we will credit your account with the value of the booking. You can use the credit for a future booking.

Do I have to at home while cleaning?

We recommend that you be at home the first time you clean it. In this way you get to know your helper personally. You can also show him the rooms and respond to your wishes. Of course, you can make an appointment or place to hand over the keys with your helper. With frequent bookings, some customers choose to leave a spare key to their cleaner.

How much do service providers earn?

The independent service providers who work with Helperland set their own payouts, ie they decide how much they earn per hour.

What support do you offer the service providers?

Our call center is available to service providers for any questions or problems regarding their bookings during office hours. Before a service provider receives orders, each individual partner receives an orientation session to familiarize themselves with the online platform and its profile.
